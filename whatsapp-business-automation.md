## Best Practices

### Response Time Goals
- Business hours: < 5 minutes
- After hours: < 2 hours next day
- Weekends: Monday morning priority

### Message Length
- Keep under 160 characters when possible
- Use line breaks for readability
- One idea per message

### Emoji Usage
- Use consistently but sparingly
- Match tone (professional but friendly)
- Test on different devices

### Personalization
- Use contact name when available
- Reference previous conversations
- Remember their business/industry
